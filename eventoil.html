DIY Essential Oil Room Spray

November30th, 2019

Join us!

Join us at La Maison and sit down with our health & beauty department experts to make an all-natural, “summer breeze” room spray with MUJI essential oil blends.

DIY Your Own Essentials!

Starting with an introduction to aromatherapy, we will guide the participants through a deeper look into MUJI’s essential oils, aroma series and the recommended blends for summer. Participants will have the chance to choose their ideal fragrance, create a unique blend, label and customize their spray bottle, then take home after the workshop.
