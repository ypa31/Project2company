Art & Tea Evenings – Watercolour Painting Workshop

December31st, 2019

‘Art & Tea Evenings’

We invite you to sit down with La Maison for a perfect ending to a long day, as we provide a place in-store to try out and practice the many different arts of the pen, brush, paper, and colour. This series aims to engage our community by bringing people together in a relaxing and meditative space to enjoy an evening of art and a warm cup of MUJI tea.

Watercolour Painting Workshop

La Maison will provide a quiet, relaxing space for you to come in to wind down with watercolour painting. Watercolour paint and brushes will be provided, and participants are welcome to come in anytime between the designated times per location below. Feel free to bring in your La Maison Sketchbook, or purchase a new one at the store!
