About us

La Maison is a Vancouver based lifestyle retail corporation that carries a variety of arts, crafts, lifestyle products for local designers, fans of DIY decorating, and minimalism.

Established in 2013, La Maison aims to bring together local craft lovers and artists to create a platform for art lovers to collaborate. Our goal is to promote environmentally friendly quality goods at an affordable price. From 100% recycled material to all organic materials, we aim for a better city for generations to come.
